Cycling and walking planner

With our journey planner, you can plan cycling and walking routes from A-B, circular leisure routes, show points of interest, and browse curated tours that help you explore our area.

Our updated planner interface is more mobile-friendly, adds waypoint functionality, an e-bike option, a walking option, 3D mode, a cycling speed control, points of interest functions, a satellite background option, and other improvements.

If you want to give us feedback on the planner, please contact us.
